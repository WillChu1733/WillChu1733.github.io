A time when I was blocked on a problem

The problem was during sprint 4's tech course where we completed the JavaScript-Kata. I was struggling through the content and although I had completed the work, I wasn't able to apply my knowledge. I broke down the question into smaller parts and approached these small questions individually. I googled tips as well and asked facilitators for help if that still didn't work. I would chatGPT to get more explanations so that I could understand the question better. I felt frustrated as I saw my classmates work and they were able to complete the questions, however, I was so stuck I didn't know where to start. I learnt to slowly break down questions into smaller parts so that it's more approachable, rather than take on the question at face value.
